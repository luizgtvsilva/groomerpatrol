Woofington's Full Groom

The most popular package for longer haired pups. This package is perfect for all dogs that need some TLC and a good tidy up! All our services are one to one and cater for any individual needs they may have, ensuring your dog gets the attention they require.
Prices will vary depending on breed and condition of your dog’s coat, please contact us for details.

Wash & Go

Does your pup love running through puddles and getting themselves in a bit of a mess? This option is great for giving them a good clean up and leaves them smelling great.





Handstripping

Handstripping is the best grooming option for course coated doggies like Border Terriers and Norfolk Terriers. It involves stripping out the dead coat on top leaving a shorter, much healthier coat. It can be a lengthy process, even for smaller breeds so for older dogs for example it may be that I suggest clipping which is quicker.
